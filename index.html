Trattoria Nova history

More than 25+ years of experiance

Trattoria Nova was born from a passion for authentic Italian flavors with a modern twist. Inspired by the traditional trattorias of Italy where meals are shared, laughter fills the air, and every dish tells a story we set out to create a space where old-world charm meets contemporary cuisine.

More than just a restaurant, Trattoria Nova is a place for connection where friends and family gather, where memories are made, and where every guest is treated like famiglia. From our warm ambiance to our attentive service, we invite you to experience Italian dining in a way that feels both familiar and excitingly new.
